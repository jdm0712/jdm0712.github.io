The History of Chelsea F.C.

Chelsea F.C.

Chelsea F.C. is an English professional soccer club based in Fulham, West London. The club competes in the Premier League, the highest level of soccer league in England. The club has won six league titles and two UEFA Champions League.

Notable Players

  • Didier Drogba (2004-2012, 2014-2015)
  • Michael Ballack (2006-2010)
  • Frank Lampard (2001-2014)
  • Eden Hazard (2012-2019)
  • John Terry (1998-2017)
